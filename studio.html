Spicy Studio is a trendsetting creative house known for edgy motion graphics and social media campaigns. They approached us to create an immersive VR experience that would showcase their most iconic projects in an interactive “virtual gallery” — blending art, animation, and narrative.

Impact & Results

  • 3x more engagement on Spicy Studio’s site
  • Featured in 5 design magazines
  • 60% increase in client inquiries
  • Audience feedback: “It feels like walking through an art dream.”

  • Transform static portfolio into an immersive VR walkthrough

  • Build an experience that works on web + VR headsets

By Adam Westwood, June 2025

In today’s digital-first world, brands compete not just for attention, but for connection. Sleek visuals and cutting-edge tech may capture the eye, but what truly lingers is the story behind the experience. At our studio, we believe the magic happens where strategy and storytelling intersect—where every pixel is purposeful, and every interaction moves the narrative forward. A flawless strategy can map out every step of the customer journey, but without emotion, it’s just a framework. Think of strategy as the blueprint of a house. It defines the structure, but without thoughtful design and character, the space feels lifeless. Similarly, a digital product that’s strategic but soulless fails to engage users on a deeper level.

Motion Capture & Motion Sciences

Built with Intent. Backed by Tech.

Complex animation often suffers from slow workflows and inconsistent data quality. The studio provides full-body, facial, and finger motion capture that produces clean, accurate data ready for direct use. This supports faster, realistic animations suitable for cinematic, gaming, and biomechanics projects. Motion analysis for sports and rehabilitation is under active development to meet emerging demands.

Virtual Production Studio

Real-time Filmmaking Revolution

Virtual production breaks down the barriers between pre-production, shooting, and post. Our LED volume combines physical sets with real-time digital environments, allowing directors, actors and crew to visualize and interact with digital content during filming. This technology enables creative decisions on-set rather than months later in post-production, resulting in more cohesive storytelling and efficient production timelines.
